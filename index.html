Welcome to UiPath Student Community AITR

UiPath is a global software company that makes robotic process automation (RPA) software. It was founded in Bucharest, Romania, by Daniel Dines and Marius Tîrcă. Its headquarters are in New York City. The company's software monitors user activity to automate repetitive front and back office tasks, including those performed using other business software such as customer relationship management or enterprise resource planning (ERP) software.

About Us

The UiPath Student Community at AITR was established in the year 2022 with the aim of fostering knowledge and skills in the field of Robotic Process Automation (RPA). Led by dedicated individuals, this community has grown steadily and has become a hub for students interested in UiPath and RPA technologies.

Community Focus

▶

The UiPath Student Community at AITR centers its activities on:

  1. RPA Education

    Providing hands-on learning experiences and workshops to equip students with practical skills in Robotic Process Automation (RPA), with a focus on UiPath.

  2. Skill Development

    Emphasizing coding challenges, project work, and simulations to enhance members' proficiency in RPA technologies, ensuring they are job-ready.

  3. Industry Integration

    Fostering collaborations with industry professionals through guest lectures, industry visits, and networking events to bridge the gap between academia and real-world applications.

  4. Project-Based Learning

    Encouraging members to engage in real-world projects, promoting creativity and problem-solving skills through hands-on experiences.

  5. Community Engagement

    Cultivating a strong sense of community through meetups, forums, and collaborative projects, facilitating knowledge exchange and mentorship among members.

  6. Continuous Learning

    Promoting a culture of continuous learning by keeping members updated on the latest RPA advancements, trends, and industry best practices through newsletters and webinars.
